How to get started

The first thing to keep in mind is that you can't be the best without the best equipment. Whatever the pros are using, probably isn't good enough!

Go to your local pro shop and find the most expensive equipment they have in stock, and ask them to order you the next level up. Spend literally every penny you have on good gear. GEAR MAKES THE GOLFER! Don't shell out for the scam lessons with the pro, if he was any good he'd be on tour. Put your money to work and buy your way to success!
